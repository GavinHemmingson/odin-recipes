Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • ½ cup melted butter/margarine
  • 24 eggs
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons salt
  • 2 ½ cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Pour melted butter into 9x13-inch baking pan
  3. Mix eggs, salt, and milk until consistent look.
  4. Bake 10 minutes. stir and bake 15 more minutes
