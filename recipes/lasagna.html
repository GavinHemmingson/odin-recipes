Lasagna

This Lasagna will elevate your taste buds to a new level. The perfect blend of ingredients that have meshed together to form a culinary masterpiece.
Whether it is made to enjoy a simple dinner with friends or to show off your cooking abilities at the community potluck it will be sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Sweet Italian Sausage, ¾lb Lean Ground Beef
  • ½ cup White Onion and 2 Cloves Garlic Crushed
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes, 2 Cans Tomato Sauce, and 2 Cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons White Sugar
  • 4 Tablespoons of Parsley, 1 ½ Teaspoon Basil Leaves, ~1 ½ Teaspoon Salt, 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning,
    ½ Teaspoon Fennel Seeds, and ¼ Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles (Store Bought or Homemade)
  • Multiple Cheeses. ¾lb Parmesan Grated, ¾lb Mozzarella Sliced, and 16oz Ricotta
  • A Single Egg

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Cook Sauasage, Ground Beef, and onions in a pot on medium heat until meat is browned
  3. Add the Tomato products with 2 tablespoons Parsley, Basil, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds,
    and Pepper into the pot and let simmer. Cover for 1 ½ hours while occasionally stirring
  4. Grab an additional pot and add water and salt to it. Bring to a boil. Add the Lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes then
    drain and rinse noodles under cold water
  5. In a seperate bowl combine Ricotta and an Egg with the remainder of Parsley and ½ Teaspoon Salt
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degree F (190 degree C)
  7. Put 1 ½ cups Meat Sauce from the pan into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    Place 6 lasagna noodles lengthwise with a little overlap. Spread half the Ricotta onto the layer of noodles
    topped with ⅓ the mozarrella cheese slices add 1 ½ cups meat sauce with ¼ cup Parmesan
  8. Repeat last step then cover with alluminium foil coated in nonstick cooking spray
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes and then remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes
  10. Pull out of oven and let rest for 15 minutes then serve.
